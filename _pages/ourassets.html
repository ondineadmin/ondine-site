Ondine Jule

The first of Ondine's vessels has arrived - welcome the Ondine Jule. A 25m breakthrough survey vessel, formerly IX-Blue Felix. The Ondine Jule was designed with vision and technology at the forefront, offering a reliable modern environmentally friendly survey platform. The Ondine Jule is young yet already mission proven vessel with an impressive track record.

The Holly

The Holly is aluminum hulled multipurpose vessel capable of a wide range of offshore survey/support operations. With a service speed of 18 Knots, The Holly is a stable and versatile platform from which to work while having the benefit of being lightweight and shallow drafted for inshore operations offering comfortable accommodation for passengers and crew. The vessel is capable of 12-hour operations, providing a cost-effective solution for a broad range of operations.
